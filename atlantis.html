Research

Our first challenge was to separate necessary information from superfluous data. Using personas from stakeholders and users, we created a design that included all of the information we deemed necessary.




Stakeholder Persona

One thing in particular that stood out from Elizabeth’s persona is the significance of the PTF pass. We decided that this piece of information should be conveyed in the environmental display in a way that is easy to understand upon first look




User Persona

From George’s persona we realized that being able to identify connections between different islands and being able to switch between different forms of transportation should be feasible and easy to do with our environmental display.




User Persona

From Patrick’s persona we found that it was important to account for travel outside of the islands. For Patrick it is also important to be able to identify connections betweens flights and other forms of transportation, particularly with regards to outer-island travel.











Wireframe & Design

The next step was to create drafts and digitized prototypes of our design. We found ourselves reworking how we wanted to display the information, and changing design elements to make the product easier to use.
